ABOUT

Spixels is the exhibition of photographs clicked by Differently abled children & adults, in Pondicherry. It’s free for the participants. The Spixels is open only for Specially Abled.

This year we had more than 200 entries. Institutions from Pondicherry, Chennai, Lucknow, Amritsar, Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Amravati & Jaipur had given us their support for Spixels among their networks.

BACKGROUND - SPIXELS

Spixels, come from two words - Special Pixels… We believe every picture has a story to tell. Every picture is made of tiny numerous pixels. The complete picture is the complete syncing of every pixel to tell a story.

Spixels is the brainchild of Ammeg Brand Management Solutions (ABMS) and is a non profit social venture. ABMS endeavours to make events like Spixels, a game changer for Specially abled. This gives them & their family a hope, an aspiration to be included not excluded.

ABMS have been helming film festivals & social events while making them socially relevant. The aim is to bring a change in the outlook of the society towards Specially abled. We @ABMS want you to stop for a minute, ponder over the idea of Specially abled, LGBTQ, Mentally ill & recovering addicts… being treated fairly & without prejudice.

ABMS believes in safer environment. We practice negligible paper use or prefer environmentally friendly or recycled materials to be used. We endeavour to our bit to leave lesser carbon footprints.

2019

The theme of 2019 year was “INCLUSIVE SOCIETY, INCLUSIVE WORKSPACE” This year we called upon people to display their sense of empathy & acceptance towards people who are not called normal by certain pre judged standards of society.

What is normal? Who are we to conclude that someone else is normal or not? Why can’t we all together create a society and a workspace where everyone is included. Let’s see the idea of inclusive workspace through the eyes of specially abled, how differently abled are being included in workspace, how they see an INCLUSIVE WORKSPACE thus leading to INCLUSIVE SOCIETY.

By this exercise of photographing a moment, a day or an instance by specially abled on this theme, will open their vision towards making change in the society & will motivate them to find a way to include differently abled in the work space, thus in the society.

We @Spixels do not believe in pitying or charity. We believe in giving a spotlight to the opportunity which might exist for bringing together everyone, without prejudice, without judgement on a common platform. Isn’t it what our age old saying is….. Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam….

TEAM

Our Media Head Shri Sanjeev Singh shares that the biggest joy his team Spixels gets is when these specially abled understand the theme of photography contest and then they go out to find the moments depicting it, thus showcasing the simple interpretation of the situations of the society.

It’s a wonderful feeling to motivate these individuals to go an extra mile to capture their vision and communicate it to the society. The memories created are lifelong.

Shri Sanjeev Singh

Shri Sanjeev Singh comes from the family of freedom fighters and national policy builders, he has an acute sense to gauge the long term needs of any section of the society and creating an atmosphere to form policies for their betterment.

He has a clear vision to incorporate differently abled people into the normal society, seamlessly.

His grounded nature always encourages everyone around him to have an open yet light hearted conversation on any subject.

The Jury of Spixels consisted of Mr Samir Sarkar (Internationally acclaimed producer of Life of Pi featured in MAMI, Rotterdam & Busan & Jonaki) and Dr Nishad Plakkal (HoD, Neonatal, JIPMER.

Samir is in Pondicherry & Mumbai based Film producer, a keen photographer and writer. His films have been appreciated internationally. He is an enthusiast when it comes to empowering Specially Abled children and bringing them unto a certain mark so that the children can also contribute to the society.

Dr. Nishad Plakkal (Photographer, Associate Professor Neonatology JIPMER, Pondicherry)

Dr Plakkal has done his medicine studies in Chandigarh, Canada and Pondicherry. Being a passionate photographer, he looks out to nature for his inspiration. Being associated with kids, he looks forward to interact with them and make the lives around him better.
